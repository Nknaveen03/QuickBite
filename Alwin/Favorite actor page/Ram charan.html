Ram charan

Prominent Indian actor | Producer | and entrepreneur

Konidela Ram Charan (born March 27, 1985) is a leading Indian actor, producer, and entrepreneur known for his work in Telugu cinema. As the son of superstar Chiranjeevi, he debuted with Chirutha (2007) and achieved massive stardom with Magadheera (2009). Renowned for his dancing and action roles, his global breakthrough came with the blockbuster RRR (2022). Wikipedia Wikipedia +2 Born in Chennai, Ram Charan has established himself as one of the highest-paid actors in India, frequently featuring in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2013. Following his acclaimed debut, he delivered several successful films, including Racha (2012), Naayak (2013), Dhruva (2016), and the critically praised Rangasthalam (2018). His performance as Alluri Sitarama Raju in S.S. Rajamouli’s RRR earned him international recognition.
